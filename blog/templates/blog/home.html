About the name

The name, Studio413, comes from the Bibile verse Phil 4:13 - "I can do anything through Christ who strengthens me." The "zen" in front of the website address is to set me apart from other Studio413's out there. Plus doing this type of art is quite zenful!


About me and my art

My art is all hand-drawn and any piece can take 5-7 days to complete. I practice what I call extreme doodling ‚Äì which is doodling with incredible attention to detail. It's in the creation of these doodles, or patterns, that causes an incredible serenity, calm, and mindfulness of being "in the flow."

Being "in the flow" means accepting what is happening in the here and now and not trying to manipulate or change it in any way. Were you ever so into something that you didn't realize how time just flew by? Chances are, you were doing something that meant so much to you that you were in the flow. And when you're in the flow, you are only mindfully aware of what you are currently doing and not thinking about doing a laundry list of other things (like laundry)! It allows you to connect with your inner self and enjoy being at piece with yourself.

Psychology in art

While not an art therapist myself, I can certainly attest to the power of art in creating a mindful, restful spirit, and that is something I want to share with you.

My art is often an extension of what is called Acceptance Commitment Therapy (ACT) and radically accepting the reality of what is, even if you don't agree with it. I will discuss this more in an upcoming post. And for my newsletter subscribers, I may include an art exercise that demonstrates how easy this is to do!
